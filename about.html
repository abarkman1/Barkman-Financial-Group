Who We Are

Independent advisors who work for you

Barkman Financial Group is an independent insurance brokerage — which means we aren't tied to any single company, quota, or product line. We shop the market for you across our top-rated carrier partners and find the best fit for your unique situation.

We specialize in serving families, retirees, and individuals who are navigating Medicare, thinking about life insurance, planning for final expenses, or looking to fill gaps in their coverage. These are big decisions — and we take them seriously.

Our clients aren't policy numbers. We remember their names, their families, and their goals. That's what being independent means to us.

Our Values

The principles that guide us

01 — Honesty

We tell you the truth

Even if it means recommending a less expensive plan. Even if it means telling you that you don't need what you thought you did. Your trust is worth more than any commission.

02 — Clarity

No jargon, no confusion

Insurance is complex by design. We translate it. By the time we're done, you'll understand exactly what you're buying, what it covers, and what it doesn't.

03 — Independence

We answer to you, not carriers

Because we work with multiple carriers, we can objectively compare options and advocate for the one that genuinely serves you best — not the one that pays us the most.

04 — Commitment

We stay in your corner

Our relationship doesn't end at enrollment. We're here for annual reviews, claim questions, coverage changes, and anything else that comes up along the way.
